FAQ

Street cleaning in Marina, answered

When is street cleaning in Marina?

Street-cleaning schedules in Marina are set block by block — each side of each street has its own day and time, so there's no single neighborhood-wide schedule. Open Marina on the CURB map to see the exact posted schedule and next sweep for any block. In practice, most Marina street-cleaning tickets are written on Tuesdays, clustered around 9am.

How much is a street-cleaning ticket in San Francisco?

As of 2026 the street-cleaning fine is $105 citywide (many older sites still list $73–97 — those are out of date). Over the last ~2 years, Marina drivers were issued 35,909 street-cleaning tickets totaling about $3.6M in fines.

How do I avoid a street-cleaning ticket in Marina?

Move your car before the posted window — most tickets here are written around 9am on Tuesdays. CURB lets you tap your block, see the next sweep, and set a free reminder (calendar or push) ~30 minutes before, plus the night before. The posted sign always wins if it differs.
